Why this project

The ZAG Chair was designed during Furniture Studio at CCAD — seating that earns its space in a room by doing two things at once. Under-seat storage built into the structure in white oak and felt, not bolted on. The full write-up — Discover, Define, Develop, Deliver — is being assembled with the same rigor as the KNOT case study.
